The Essentials for Optimizing Your Skincare Routine✨

Your guide to helping you glow!

Basics

Before diving in, ensure you're following these core steps:

  • Cleanser
  • Moisturizer
  • SPF (Sun Protection Factor)

Three Key Steps to Enhancing Your Routine

1. Exfoliation

Adding an exfoliating step will help you with:

  • Sloughing off dead skin buildup
  • Increasing product penetration
  • Stimulating collagen and elastin production

2. Antioxidants

Antioxidants will protect your skin from damage caused by UV exposure and pollution. This will help you with:

  • Neutralizing free radicals
  • Promoting collagen and elastin production
  • Keeping skin cells healthy

3. Targeted Treatments

If you struggle from specific skin concerns like acne, wrinkles, or hyperpigmentation:

  • Try using a retinoid or an AHA exfoliant
  • If retinoids are too harsh, focus on using an AH instead
  • If you'd like to incorporate both into your routine, alternate with using AHAs in the morning and retinoids at night

Recommended Daily Routines

A.M. Routine:

(in this order)

  1. Cleanser
  2. Antioxidant
  3. Moisturizer
  4. Sunscreen

P.M. Routine:

(in this order)

  1. Cleanser
  2. Retinoid or AHA
  3. Moisturizer

Frequency and Application Tips

Here's some tips for maximizing your routine:

  • Use retinoids 4-5 nights a week for optimal results
  • Use AHA exfoliants 2-3x week or as frequently as your skin can tolerate
  • Monitor skin tolerance for redness or irritation
  • Monitor skin tolerance: If you experience redness, dryness, peeling, or irritation, reduce frequency or consider alternative products

Physical Exfoliation

  • Try incorporating a physical exfoliant 2-3 times a week, morning or night (after cleansing). This can be used in the same routine as a chemical exfoliant or retinol
  • Helps buff off dead skin cells
  • Enhances skin radiance, smooths texture, and increases product penetration

Budget Considerations

If you're on a budget, prioritize antioxidants and retinoids for the biggest impact. These "heavy hitters" will have the most significant impact on your results.

Key Takeaways

  • Consistency is crucial: Daily small actions make the biggest long-term difference
  • Expect nonlinear results: You may experience highs and lows, especially when starting new products
  • Skincare is a journey: Like fitness, skincare requires consistent effort for lasting results
